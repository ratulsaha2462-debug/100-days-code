The #100DaysOfCode challenge keeps me accountable. Every day for 100 days, I build something — no matter how small. Each project lives on GitHub with its own README, live demo, and learnings.

The rule is simple: code for at least 1 hour, push a commit, and share it publicly. No excuses. No breaks. Just consistent progress.
